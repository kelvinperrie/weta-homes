Instructions

There are three different types of weta houses you can build depending on how much effort you want to put in.

  1. Easy weta house
    This one doesn’t need a hinge or a hook but is maybe not as tough. You can build it without a clear plastic window (but when you open the door the wetas might fall out).
  2. Weta motel
    This one needs a bit more stuff than the easy one (a hinge and a hook). If you don’t have a router to cut out an area for the clear plastic window you can skip that step and instead just put packing around the edges of the plastic window to keep the light out.
  3. Branch weta house
    This is similar to the above house but is made out of a branch! It is a little bit harder to make because you have to cut the branch in half.

Tips

  • You can get untreated planks of wood from old pallets. Stores (like bunnings or mitre 10) will leave these outside for anyone to pick up.
  • Anything metal like nails, screws, hinges, or hooks should be galvanised (or zinc plated) so that they won’t rust so easily.
  • Clear plastic sheets for the windows when you open the doors - you can buy these from hardware stores (we got ours from bunnings). To cut these put a straight edge on top of it and then score it with a knife (a lot) so that it has a deep mark in it. Then put it over the top of a straight edge and push down to snap it.
